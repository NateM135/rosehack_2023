Working Women

Working Women is an online training, job-matching, and coaching program that aims to increase employment for young women. This data-driven application utilizes demographic data specific to the user's location to provide targeted job suggestions and resources.

Enter your country and region of residence to interact with data visualizations for industry composition, job growth rate per sector, and employed persons per industry. Then, choose your desired job interest to get placed into a cohort and matched with an industry mentor.
